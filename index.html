Narendra Modi

Narendra Modi is the current Prime Minister of India, serving since 2014. He was previously the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014.

Early Life and Education

Modi was born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, India. He received a degree in political science from Gujarat University in 1983.

Career

Modi began his political career as a volunteer in the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) and later became a full-time worker for the organization. He served in various capacities within the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) before being elected as the Chief Minister of Gujarat in 2001.

Prime Minister of India

Modi was elected as the Prime Minister of India in 2014 and was re-elected in 2019. During his tenure, he has launched several initiatives including the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, Make in India, and Digital India.
